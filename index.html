Most Popular

Full Grooming

Your pup walks out looking and smelling like a brand new dog. Our groomers tailor every cut to your pet's breed, coat type, and your personal preferences, whether you want a classic breed-standard clip or a custom teddy bear trim.

Each full groom starts with a warm bath using premium shampoos matched to your dog's skin and coat needs, followed by hand blow-drying (never cage drying), a precise haircut, ear cleaning, nail trimming, and a spritz of finishing cologne. We also check for skin irritations, lumps, or tangles so you stay informed about your pet's health between vet visits. Most sessions take 2–3 hours depending on breed and coat condition.

Starting at $65 · Varies by breed & coat

  • Bath with premium shampoo & conditioner
  • Breed-specific haircut & styling
  • Hand blow-dry (never cage dried)
  • Ear cleaning, nail trim & finishing touches
  • Skin & coat health check
  • Finishing cologne & bandana
Between Grooms

Bath & Brush

Perfect for keeping your pet fresh and clean between full grooming appointments. A bath and brush is ideal for short-coated breeds that don't need a haircut, or for any dog that just needs a reset after a muddy week at the park.

We start with a deep-cleaning bath using hypoallergenic shampoo suited to your dog's skin type, followed by a full hand blow-dry and thorough brush-out to remove loose fur and prevent matting. Every bath and brush also includes ear cleaning, nail trimming, and a light sanitary trim around the paws and face. It's the fastest way to get your dog looking and smelling great without a full haircut. Most visits take about an hour.

Starting at $40 · Varies by breed & coat

  • Hypoallergenic shampoo bath
  • Hand blow-dry & thorough brushing
  • Ear cleaning & nail trim included
  • Light sanitary trim (paws & face)
  • De-shedding for heavy shedders
Premium Care

Specialty Treatments

Targeted treatments for pets with sensitive skin, allergies, heavy shedding, or coats that need extra love. Whether your dog is dealing with dry, flaky skin or a thick double coat that won't stop shedding, we have solutions that go beyond a standard bath.

Our oatmeal and medicated baths soothe irritated skin and help manage conditions like hot spots and dermatitis. Deep conditioning treatments restore moisture and shine to brittle or damaged coats. For heavy shedders like Huskies, German Shepherds, and Golden Retrievers, our de-shedding treatment removes loose undercoat and reduces shedding at home by up to 80%. We also offer de-matting for dogs with tangled or neglected coats, done slowly and gently to keep your pet comfortable throughout.

Starting at $20 add-on · Standalone from $55

  • Oatmeal & medicated baths
  • Deep conditioning treatments
  • De-shedding & de-matting services
  • Hot spot & sensitive skin relief
  • Flea & tick treatment baths
Add-On Services

Nail Care

Overgrown nails can cause pain, affect your dog's posture, and even lead to joint problems over time. We offer both nail trimming and Dremel grinding for a smooth, comfortable finish. Grinding is especially helpful for dogs with dark nails where the quick is hard to see, and for pets who are sensitive to the pressure of clippers. We go slow and steady. Most dogs barely notice.

From $15

Teeth Brushing

Dental disease is one of the most common health issues in dogs, and it starts with plaque buildup. Our teeth brushing add-on uses pet-safe enzymatic toothpaste to remove surface buildup, freshen breath, and support gum health between veterinary dental cleanings. It's a quick step that makes a big difference, especially for smaller breeds prone to dental problems like Yorkies, Chihuahuas, and Dachshunds.

From $10

Ear & Gland Care

Ear infections and impacted anal glands are uncomfortable for your pet and easy to miss at home. Our groomers gently clean ears to remove wax and debris that can lead to infection, especially important for floppy-eared breeds like Cocker Spaniels and Basset Hounds. Anal gland expression is done externally by experienced hands, relieving discomfort and preventing the scooting and odor that comes with full glands.

From $10 each

Yes, We Groom Cats Too

Cat Grooming

Finding a groomer who works with cats is hard. Most salons won't touch them. At A Passion For Pets, we've been grooming cats since 2007 and our team knows how to handle even the most anxious or feisty felines with calm, confident care.

Every cat session is done at a pace your cat can tolerate with no rushing and no stress. If your cat has severely matted fur, we'll work through it gently or recommend a lion cut to give them a fresh, comfortable start.

  • Lion cuts & sanitary trims
  • Comb-outs & de-matting
  • Nail trimming for cats
  • Long-hair breeds: Persians, Maine Coons & more

About Us
