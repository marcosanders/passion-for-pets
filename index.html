About Us

Treating Every Pet Like Family Since 2007

A Passion For Pets isn't just a name — it's our promise. Founded in 2007, our salon in the Village Center Shopping Center has been Gilbert's go-to grooming destination for nearly two decades.

Our team of 6 experienced groomers each bring 15 to 30 years of hands-on experience. Led by Shelly, who customers call "a true artist," we specialize in breed-specific styling and work with patience and compassion on every pet that walks through our door.

From nervous first-timers to senior dogs who need a little extra care, we adapt our approach to make every visit comfortable and stress-free.
